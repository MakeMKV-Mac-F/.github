## What Is MakeMKV for Mac

makemkv for mac is a format converter — a "transcoder" — built for one job: turning the video on your own
Blu-ray and DVD discs into MKV files that play almost anywhere. Rather than re-encoding footage, it pulls
the video, audio, chapters and meta-information out of the disc and repacks them into a single open
container, so nothing is recompressed and nothing is lost. The result is a movie you fully own, in a format
that keeps working long after the disc is scratched or misplaced.

## Ripping Your Own Discs

The core function of makemkv mac is reading a disc and writing an MKV file for each title. Insert the disc,
let the app scan its titles, pick what you want, and start — there are no codec menus or bitrate sliders to
wrestle with. Because it reads directly from the disc, download makemkv for mac does the ripping and
converting in one pass, so a disc on your shelf becomes a usable file without a separate tool for each step.

## Keeping Everything Intact

What sets makemkv for mac apart is how little it throws away. A lossy converter might quietly drop a
commentary or a dubbed language; this one keeps them all by default. Every audio and video track survives,
including HD audio, and chapter markers carry over so scene-skipping still works. Track-language and
audio-type tags stay attached too, which matters when a disc ships with several audio or subtitle options.
Since nothing is re-encoded, the MKV is a faithful copy of the disc — this is what makes dvd to mkv mac an
archival tool rather than a shortcut.
